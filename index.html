Jonny Huber is a student at De La Salle High School. He is a sophomore and plays lacrosse and hockey for the school. He also has had an internship at the Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory and at Uptempo Group-a software development company. Jonny currently works for Uptempo Tech where he teaches kids tech skills like working with CAD software, 3D Printing, and Coding.

PHOTOSHOP EDIT 1

In this edit in Photoshop, the hood ordament of the car was edited to remove the worse looking parts of the arrow and the man. I used the spot healing brush tool and the patch tool.

PHOTOSHOP EDIT 2

In this edit in Photoshop, the multiple pictures of Monterey Bay Aquarium are combined in a collage and a rectangle with the words "Monterey, California" cut out.

PHOTOSHOP EDIT 3

In this edit in Photoshop I took two different images and combined them to make it look like the car was orignially on that lawn.

TYPOGRAPHY 1

This design of the word "create" was done in Adobe Illustrator. I made a ruler in Illustrator and then made each leter from multiple rulers.

TYPOGRAPHY 2

This design of the word "create" was also done in Adobe Illustrator. I made the word "create" to look like it was being 3D Printed.

CEREAL DRIVE POSTER

This poster was designed in Adobe Illustrator for the yearly cereal drive for people in need. I designed the bowl, car, and spoon from scratch. I did this by taking shpaes like cicles, rectangles, and lines.

CUBISM

This poster was designed in Adobe Illustrator as a flyer for the games for a hockey team. Jonny made the background, hockey sticks, and puck by making the outline of the things, then dragging lines randomly around, and finally filling in the shapes that were created with different colors.

LOGOS

These logos were made in Adobe Illustrator. They are Wordmark, Symbol, and Combination in color and black and white varients.
